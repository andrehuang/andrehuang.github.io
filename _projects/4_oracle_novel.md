A long novel I'm planning — not a draft yet, only a framework. Working title 《神谕》 (*Oracle*); candidate series umbrella 《空和平》／《长停战》 (*The Hollow Peace* / *The Long Armistice*).

Set ten years after a "hollow peace" treaty. Two civilizational blocs — a Mediterranean-and-New-World *Greater Hellas* and a Eurasian-Persian-and-East *Greater Persia / Heavenly Courts* — face each other across a war-scarred transition zone, with an ungovernable steppe to the north and a non-aligned mercantile confederation to the south. An expanding oracular network grows uncannily precise; in parallel, more and more people "awaken" — some die of bliss, others become entities no polity can absorb. A larger crisis is coming, but every character can only see a fragment of it.

The framework v0 — themes, world map, factions, characters, structure — lives at [/assets/html/oracle-novel-framework.html](/assets/html/oracle-novel-framework.html). It's written in Chinese and will be reworked many times before any prose exists.
