A 3-month, project-based course designed for a middle-schooler. The student writes no code, but ships a playable PVP arena, a complete game mode, an AI teammate bot, and finally a real Minecraft mod published online.

The full case study — design, field notes, toolkit, and open questions — lives at [/assets/html/case01-ai-minecraft.html](/assets/html/case01-ai-minecraft.html). It's a living document, written in Chinese, and updates as the course progresses.
